Fixed Window rate limiter is the simplest form of rate limiting. It means that once the expiration has been set, a client that reaches the limit will be blocked from making further requests until the expiration time arrives. If a client has a limit of 50 requests every minute and makes all 50 requests in the first 5 seconds of the minute, it will have to wait 55 seconds to make another request.
